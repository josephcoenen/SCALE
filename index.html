WHAT IS SCALE?

SCALE is a modular, off-grid infrastructure system designed to reclaim urban safety through the Energy-Safety Nexus. By providing a solar-powered steel framework, we empower communities to transform dark public spaces into resilient, self-managed hubs for work, light, and connectivity. It is a tactical blueprint for decentralized urban growth and social autonomy.
